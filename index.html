NOKYO SUSHI

Sushi is a common cuisine found in many countries. It originated in Japan and is considered to be a highly artistic and skilled type of food from the country. Sushi uses fish, both raw and cooked, vegetables, and vinegared rice. Common side garnishes include soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.

SASHIMI

Sashimi is raw fish, served in long rectangular slices known as hira-zukuri.Wasabi, soy sauce, and pickled ginger are also commonly served as garnish. Essentially, sashimi is deconstructed simple sushi rolls.

CHIRASHI

You will be served a bowl of vinegared rice topped with raw fish slices and different garnishes. The fish is typically chosen by the chef to offer you a surprise element. Fruit and vegetables are sometimes also included in this dish.

URAMAKI

The rice is on the outside of the nori rather than the inside. The nori will be wrapped around the filling. It often comes with lots of toppings and sauces that you can dip the pieces into.

DRAGON ROLL

Fillings with tempura-fried shrimp, cucumber, and avocado. They are also covered in a thin slice of avocado and drizzled with spicy mayonnaise and unagi sauce.
